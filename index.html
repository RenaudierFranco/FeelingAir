About Us

We are a non-scheduled air transport company dedicated to designing each trip tailored to our clients. We hold all certifications required by the Argentine aeronautical authority (ANAC) for both our aircraft and crew (135). We have a fleet that allows us to operate in all authorized airports and unprepared runways in the country, always ensuring safety, privacy, and comfort.

Quality and Comfort

We provide a unique and personalized flight experience. Experience the elegance in an affordable way and enjoy a journey that reflects the best in comfort and technology.
